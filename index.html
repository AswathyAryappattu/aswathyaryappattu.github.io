About

I'm a robotics and systems engineer passionate about building intelligent machines. My work spans the full stack — from writing firmware for microcontrollers to designing perception pipelines for autonomous robots.

I enjoy the challenge of making hardware and software work together reliably, whether that's tuning a PID controller in MATLAB, building a ROS node in C++, or prototyping a sensor circuit on Arduino.

Currently open to new opportunities in robotics, embedded systems, and autonomous systems.
